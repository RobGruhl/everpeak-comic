Prismor

Blue Crystal Dragonborn Paladin

Class: Paladin (Oath of the Ancients) Race: Blue Crystal Dragonborn Age: Middle decades (mature)

Background

Prismor is older than many who now call Everpeak home. Approaching his middle decades, he came to the citadel decades ago after hearing tales of a place where the old magics of vanished high elves lingered in the very stones.

He settled here to fulfill his paladin's vow: to defend this citadel born of sacrifice and mystery. His long service has made him a mentor to younger settlers and a trusted guardian of Everpeak's fragile harmony.

Personality

Calm, protective, and steadfast. Prismor carries himself with noble bearing and quiet wisdom. He is deeply dedicated to defending Everpeak and upholding his sacred oaths.
