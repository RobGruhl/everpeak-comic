About This Project

Everpeak Citadel: Echoes of the Dawn's Crown is an AI-generated fantasy comic based on a Dungeons & Dragons campaign. The story follows Val, Prismor, Apocalypse Winter (Pocky), Lunara, and Malrik as they uncover a conspiracy threatening the magical citadel during the rare Dawn's Crown alignment.

This project combines the narrative richness of tabletop roleplaying with cutting-edge AI image generation technology to create a unique visual storytelling experience.

Development Process

The creation workflow for this comic involves several steps:

  • Script Writing: Converting D&D campaign notes into structured comic book narrative
  • Character & Location Design: Developing detailed visual descriptions for consistency
  • JSON Structuring: Breaking down each page into panels with complete metadata
  • AI Generation: Using OpenAI's image generation with carefully crafted prompts
  • Optional Refinement: Enhancing selected panels with Google Gemini
  • Page Assembly: Compositing panels into full pages with adaptive layouts
  • Web Optimization: Converting to WebP format for fast online reading

Behind the Scenes

Each panel begins with a detailed prompt that includes:

  • Complete character descriptions (appearance, clothing, equipment)
  • Location atmosphere and environmental details
  • Scene composition and character positioning
  • Dialogue and thought bubble placement
  • Comic art style directives (bold ink work, vibrant colors)

The structured approach ensures character consistency across panels while allowing the AI to bring each scene to life with unique artistic interpretation.
