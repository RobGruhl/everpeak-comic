The Elven Sanctum

Ancient, powerful, sacred

Ancient high elven architecture with carved stone pillars, vaulted ceiling covered in constellation maps and celestial charts. Five alcoves housing the True Lenses for elemental essences. Ruins and scars from century-old battle visible on walls and floor. Cathedral-like scale with reverent atmosphere. Medieval high fantasy sanctum with high elven precision engineering.
