## MAJOR LOCATIONS - VISUAL DESCRIPTIONS

### **Everpeak Citadel - Exterior**

**Overall Impression:**
- Massive mountain fortress carved from white stone
- Multiple crystalline towers reaching skyward
- Built atop converging ley lines (visible as faint glowing lines in mountain)
- Snow-capped peaks surrounding
- Ancient high elven architecture mixed with newer additions
- Magical lights and lanterns visible even in daylight

**Key Visual Elements:**
- Grand main courtyard where Yule Tree ceremony occurs
- Terrace gardens clinging to impossible ledges
- Winding paths and staircases carved into mountain
- Airship docking platforms (currently unused in winter)
- Festival decorations everywhere - ribbons, lanterns, banners
- Smoke from many chimneys and forges

---

### **The Courier Tunnels**

**Appearance:**
- Rough-hewn stone passages
- Chalk runes and symbols covering every surface
- Different colored chalk marks showing different routes
- Some runes glow faintly with magical energy
- Cramped, winding, maze-like
- Occasional magical trap doors and hidden passages
- Dim magical lighting (cold blue glow)
- Some areas show displacement magic gone wrong - spatial distortions

**Important Details:**
- Smudged and tampered runes in key locations
- "Danger Do Not Go" warnings in various colors
- Mysterious passages sealed off
- Signs of recent tampering

---

### **The Balcony Garden Café**

**Appearance:**
- Impossible location - sunny terrace high in snow-covered mountains
- Lush greenery everywhere - vines, flowers, herbs
- Exotic plants that shouldn't grow at this altitude
- Small cozy café building, rustic wood construction
- Tables and chairs scattered among the plants
- Glass walls showing spectacular mountain views
- Magical warmth despite altitude and season
- Coffee and tea preparation area visible
- Rich soil in garden beds, clearly magical in nature

**Atmosphere:**
- Peaceful, serene sanctuary
- Smells of coffee, herbs, flowers
- Warm despite surroundings
- Hidden away from main citadel

---

### **The Grand Library**

**Appearance:**
- Soaring ceilings supported by ornate columns
- Columns carved with runes and magical symbols
- Endless shelves stretching floor to ceiling
- Rolling ladders and spiral staircases everywhere
- Tall windows filtering pale winter sunlight
- Reading desks with magical lights
- Ancient manuscripts in protective cases
- Star charts and astronomical diagrams on walls
- Dust motes floating in light shafts
- Smell of old parchment and ink

**Key Features:**
- Marge's orderly workspace near entrance
- Hidden section with rare/powerful texts
- Mezzanine leading to observatory

---

### **The Observatory**

**Appearance:**
- Small domed chamber accessed from library mezzanine
- Glass ceiling showing sky (stars during finale)
- Ancient magical telescope at center
- Brass and crystal construction
- Celestial maps and charts on walls
- Forgotten, dusty - recently restored
- Gentle starlight filtering through dome

---

### **The Elven Sanctum (Final Battle Location)**

**Appearance:**
- Hidden cavern deep beneath citadel
- Carved stone walls with elegant high elven designs
- Soft glows of multiple colors - emerald (nature), silver-blue (mechanistic), lavender (celestial), golden (harmony), and shifting prisms (displacement)
- The Orrery at center:
  - Magnificent structure of crystal lenses suspended in air
  - Metallic arms holding lenses in precise positions
  - Connected by glowing runic lines
  - Hums with power
  - Delicate framework suggesting both science and magic
  - Lenses refract light in complex patterns
- Circular aperture in ceiling for Dawn's Crown alignment
- Raised dais where Lord Alric stands
- Ancient, sacred atmosphere
- Chamber size: Large enough for combat but intimate
- Carved benches along walls (long unused)
